FAQ

How do I import photos?
Tap the import button on the photo wall, or drag a folder into the app.
What formats does DuoImage support?
SBS stereo files in JPG, PNG, HEIC, TIFF, WebP, BMP, and PSD formats.
Does DuoImage collect my data?
No. All processing happens on-device. No account required, no data collected.
